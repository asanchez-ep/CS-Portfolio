Vindinium

Vindinium is known as an "Artificial Intelligence programming challenge". Essentially, people code bots so that they can fight with other people's AI bots while trying to have the greatest possesion of Gold. The winner bot of a tournament is determined by what bot has the greatest ownership of Gold at the end of 1200 turns.

Bot asanz

Asanz is the name of the bot I created, and I chose to not make my bot super agressive. My bot has three functions: attacking other bots that have more than 1 mine, going to the nearest tavern when its health is lower than 40, and acquring any free mines.
